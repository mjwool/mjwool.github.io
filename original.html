At Merino&Jumbuck, our philosophy is to create beautiful, pure Australian merino wool thermal and active wear of superior quality.

Based in Melbourne, Merino&Jumbuck is Australia’s largest manufacturer of pure merino wool thermal garments. Our distinctly Australian merino wool thermal and active wear has been much loved for generations.

Our appreciation for Australia’s unique environment and our passion for all things merino can be recognised in the exquisite quality of our products. Merino wool produces the softest, finest, most luxurious yarn in the world. With its exceptional softness, natural breathability and its ability to wick moisture away from the skin, merino wool is the perfect, versatile fibre for all seasons.

Beautiful, pure Australian merino wool allows us to create silky smooth, high performance active wear and next-to-skin thermal garments that are functional, adaptable and remarkably luxurious.

Merino&Jumbuck’s superior selection of designs and products is inspired by the miracle of nature and brought to life by us, so that we can share the exceptional benefits of this remarkable natural fibre with you.
